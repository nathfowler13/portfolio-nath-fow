About

My Story

Hello. I am an aspiring UX Designer that began my journey after coming across a social media ad. Just let me explain real quick.
For the past 7 years, I have been in the biotechnology space working in either manufacturing or late-stage R&D and validation. In both sectors, I found my flow when working through a set protocol. As I began to mature in my career, I learned to look up and ask, is there a better way to do this? Around the same time, a social media ad for a career change to UX found me. Wanting to know what the heck UX is, I found myself reading through articles and listening to podcasts to learn more about the field. I began to recognize an overlap in my personal values and a tenet of UX: to serve on behalf of others.
I am excited that I have embarked on this journey of life-long learning, and I look forward to collaborating with design teams, users, and business stakeholders to create lasting, well-designed user experiences.
